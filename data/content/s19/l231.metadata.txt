summary: St. Melangell lived a solitary life in the Montgomeryshire mountains, avoiding marriage arranged by her father, and befriending animals for fifteen years before meeting Prince Brochwel during a hare hunt. Impressed by her holiness and the miraculous events surrounding her, the Prince granted her land to found a sanctuary, where Melangell protected hares and continued her devout life.
