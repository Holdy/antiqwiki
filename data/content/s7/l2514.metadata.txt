summary: Hyvarnion, a minstrel in King Childebert's palace, dreams of a maiden, Rivanone, whom he eventually finds and marries, but their joy is marred by their son Hervé being born blind, soon followed by Hyvarnion's death. Despite hardships, Hervé becomes a famed minstrel guided by his loyal animals, eventually creating a sanctuary of music and love, symbolizing the kingdom of song inherited from his parents.
