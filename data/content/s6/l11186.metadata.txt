summary: In the Flemish town of Quesnoy, a cruel lord named Burchard attempts to force a peasant girl, Renelde, to marry him after she refuses to leave her grandmother to work at his castle. Renelde is later tasked with spinning a shroud for Burchard, whose health declines as she does; after he repents, she completes his shroud, leading to his death and finally, uniting with her true love, Guilbert.
