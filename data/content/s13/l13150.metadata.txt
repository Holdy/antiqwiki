summary: Umatilla, a peaceful Native American chief at the Columbia Cascades, entrusts his son Benjamin to a white schoolteacher, and as the boy falls to a plague, he encourages his tribe to seek friendship with the settlers. After Benjamin's death, Umatilla persuades the tribe to promise peace, and then dies beside his son, leading to a lasting harmony with the white settlers.
