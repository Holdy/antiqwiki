summary: In New Milford, Connecticut, a love story unfolds as Chief Waramaug's daughter, Lillinonah, falls for a white man she nurses back to health, only for him to leave with a promise to return. When her lover finally returns, it is too late; Lillinonah, betrothed to another, chooses to end her life in the river, where they both ultimately perish after a desperate but failed attempt to reunite.
