summary: In Mauch Chunk, Pennsylvania, the young chief Onoko and his wife Wenonah's happiness and success incite the envy of Mitche Manitou, who creates a chasm in the mountains, leading to the tragic death of the couple as the lake rushes through the gap. Their doomed love story is immortalized in the name of a nearby glen and cascade, while Manitou's jealousy reminds all that excessive happiness invites the wrath of the gods.
