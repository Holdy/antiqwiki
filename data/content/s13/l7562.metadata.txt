summary: The tale of Heartbreak Hill tells of a sailor who left behind an Agawam maiden in Ipswich, promising to return, but his ship eventually wrecked in a storm as she watched helplessly from the hill. Heartbroken and deprived of hope, the maiden soon passed away, her spirit lingering over the site where she awaited her lover's return.
