summary: A misunderstanding at the Manitou springs between a Shoshone and a Comanche hunter over drinking rights escalated into a deadly confrontation, leading to the drowning of the Shoshone chief, Ausaqua, by the Comanche, Wacomish. The incident, witnessed by a spirit, resulted in a curse on the Comanche and a division between the tribes that erupted into widespread warfare across the region.
