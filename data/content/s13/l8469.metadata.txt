summary: In the winter of 1750-51, the ship Palatine, filled with Dutch merchants and emigrants, faced tragedy as a rebellious crew abandoned the passengers after stealing their money, leaving them to die, after which the ship was set ablaze off Block Island, creating a spectral image seen years later. This ghostly sight and echoes of a woman's screams hauntingly reappeared annually, terrifying the islanders, symbolizing the ship's doomed fate and unearthly vengeance.
