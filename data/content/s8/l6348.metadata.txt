summary: In Wales, the legend of the jack-o'-lantern involves Sion Dafydd tricking a devil multiple times, eventually escaping by clinging to an apple tree and transforming into a fairy. Sion's cleverness and the devil's stupidity are central to the tale, which ties his transformation to the notorious village of Rhiwgyfylchi, known for its wickedness.
