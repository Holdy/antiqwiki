summary: On Midsummer Eve in 1878, Druids and bards at Pontypridd held a traditional feast celebrating the summer solstice, where they performed ancient rites and initiated new members. Welsh traditions also speak of the mysterious Snake-stone, formed from the sociable hissing of snakes, believed to bring prosperity to its finder.
