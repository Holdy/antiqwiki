summary: John Newton was unjustly executed for highway robbery after being wrongly accused and, before his death, claimed that his grave would remain grassless as a testament to his innocence. For thirty years, the spot at Montgomery Castle graveyard remained bare, defying interventions to plant grass, in accordance with Newton's final prophetic words.
