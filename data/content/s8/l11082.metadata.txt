summary: In Carmarthenshire, Wales, the ancient Canna's Stone, associated with healing rituals connected to a magical well, attracts folklore of miraculous relocations conducted by creatures and self-locomoting stones. Similar stories of stones with supernatural powers, including ones in Builth and the Isle of Anglesea, suggest a widespread belief in the mystical transient properties of such stones across Wales.
