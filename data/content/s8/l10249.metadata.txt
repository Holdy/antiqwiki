summary: Sul Coffa is a Welsh tradition involving respectful visits to graves after a funeral, demonstrating both collective mourning and the personal aspect of honoring the dead. Ivan the Harper requested a unique Sul Coffa with musical performances, which was faithfully executed by his disciples at his graveside.
