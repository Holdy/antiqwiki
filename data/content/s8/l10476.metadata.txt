summary: A legendary bell known for its melodious sound was stolen by a king but was miraculously returned to St. Illtyd's church in Wales, leading a procession of horses along the way. Years later, a bell discovered in Llantwit Major was believed to be connected to this legend, reflecting the lasting veneration of St. Illtyd, a former soldier of King Arthur who founded churches.
