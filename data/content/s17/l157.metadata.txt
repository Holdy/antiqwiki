summary: A mysterious, beautiful woman regularly attended the church in Zennor, captivating the village with her appearance and singing voice, before disappearing with local young man Mathey Trewella, who never returned. Years later, a ship's captain reported seeing a mermaid in the sea who asked him to move his anchor, revealing she was Mathey's wife, which led the Zennor villagers to create a carving commemorating the tale in their church.
