summary: John Jones and his wife from Penlon cottage were unable to churn milk into butter due to a curse placed by an enemy, leading them to seek help from a wizard known as the "Dyn Hysbys" in Cwrt-y-Cadno. After failing the first time due to revealing the charm paper, a second attempt with a new paper, procured by a neighbor and kept secret, successfully broke the curse, allowing them to produce butter.
