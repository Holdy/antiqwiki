summary: In West Wales, it is believed that fits can be cured by wearing a band made of hair from an ass's shoulder, while hair from an ass cut at midnight is thought to cure quinsy. Various other charms, such as carrying a potato for rheumatism or consuming a buried penny roll for whooping cough, illustrate the folk remedies practiced in the region.
