summary: The belief in omens surrounding animals is varied, as a white cock is considered either lucky or unlucky depending on the person, and its daytime crow is associated with the arrival of a friend. In contrast, a crow at night is viewed ominously, often signifying death, and historically, cockfighting once took place in parts of Wales like Carmarthenshire and Cardiganshire.
