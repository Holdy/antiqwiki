summary: Tea-cup divination, popular among young girls in Wales, involves interpreting tea leaves to predict future events related to love and marriage, with signs like a ring indicating an imminent wedding and two hearts suggesting a forthcoming marriage. While some women rely on experts for guidance, many can discern meanings themselves, using symbols like lilies and hearts to foresee happiness or misfortune in romantic affairs.
