summary: Near Tre'rddol in North Cardiganshire, Morris, a notorious robber, lived in a cave where he stole hens and sheep until he was eventually caught and hanged in Cardigan. Another account from Pembrokeshire tells of a robber named Morris, who trained a dog to fetch arrows, and was ultimately killed and buried at a site marked by 'Bedd Morris' stone.
