summary: Superstitions in Wales suggest that various signs, such as a cock crowing before midnight or a hen behaving unusually, predict death either in the family or nearby. Other ominous signs include a persistent screeching owl, a howling dog, or even a burnt piece of clothing foretelling a loss, as experienced by a farmer's wife near Aberystwyth.
