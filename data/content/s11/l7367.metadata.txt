summary: In the parish of Llangeler in Carmarthenshire, beginning on Pentecost Thursday, May 21, 1719, an unseen spirit started hurling stones at people working in the fields, continuing with increasing force over several days. Despite numerous witnesses and daily incidents, the source of the stone throwing remained unseen and mysterious, causing fear and wonder among the locals until the phenomena eventually ceased.
