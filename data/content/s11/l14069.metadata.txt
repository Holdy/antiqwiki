summary: In Wales, 'clefyd y Galon' or love-sickness is a common ailment, especially among young women who have been jilted, with a secret cure passed down by old practitioners like Ned y Wain near Aberystwyth. This secret involves a mystical measuring method to diagnose and potentially cure the afflicted, which is revealed in the story as a relic of Wales' dark days for the amusement of readers.
