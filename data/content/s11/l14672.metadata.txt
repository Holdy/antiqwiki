summary: Ffynon Pwllffein was a famous well on the side of the river Clettwr in Llandyssul, celebrated for its healing virtues until it was destroyed by the river. Another well, Ffynon-Ddewi, near Alltyrodyn, was also well-known for curing whooping-cough and was visited within living memory.
