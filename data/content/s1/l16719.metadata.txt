summary: The Phynnodderee, a Manks spirit expelled from fairy society for courting a human, is known in the Isle of Man for helping with chores, such as moving large stones and gathering hay, until made to disappear after receiving clothes as a reward. This spirit, who originally played in the merry glen of Rushen, left with a lament when given clothes, signifying his loss of connection to his cherished Glen Rushen.
