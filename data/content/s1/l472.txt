                         THE FAIRY MYTHOLOGY




                            INTRODUCTION.

      In oldè dayès of the King Artoúr,
      Of which that Bretons spoken gret honoúr,
      All was this lond fulfilled of faërie;
      The elf-qrene with hir jolie companie
      Danced full oft in many a grenè mede.
                                        CHAUCER.
