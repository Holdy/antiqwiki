summary: Brownie, a helpful household spirit in Scottish folklore, would secretly assist families with chores but leave if acknowledged or offered overt gifts. Various tales exist about Brownie, including his departure from Leithin-hall after being offered new clothes and a similar occurrence at Bodsbeck Ha', resulting in their fortunes reversing.
