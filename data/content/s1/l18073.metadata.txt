summary: In the valley of Goel, a man named Peric joins the mythical Korred in their dance and, after impressing them, is rewarded with the removal of his hump and a change in his hair color. A fellow tailor, Jean, attempts the same feat but demands riches; however, he is punished by being given Peric's old hump and red hair for his lack of creativity and greed.
