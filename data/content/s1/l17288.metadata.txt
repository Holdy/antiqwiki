summary: The tale revolves around a spirit known as the 'Spirit of the Van,' whose departure from Van Pool was marked by a significant event involving a ploughshare, leaving her family legacy of skilled doctors behind. This legend contributes to the Welsh fairy lore, with a focus on the Jones family of Muddfi, who were renowned for producing exceptional doctors, believed to originate from the tradition of the seventh son being destined for healing.
