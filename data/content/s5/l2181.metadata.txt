summary: In a tale explaining cultural differences, a shrewd young man from the north marries a lone woman from the southern region, only for the two to be torn apart and each half transformed into a new woman. The resulting women from the north and south inherit distinct traits from their wooden replacements, leading to northern women becoming expert needlewomen and southern women becoming skilled dancers.
