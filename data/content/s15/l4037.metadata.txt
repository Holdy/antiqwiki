summary: Robert Hunter, a shepherd from Syderstone, Norfolk, was alarmed one night in March 1832 by loud, lamentable groans coming from the unoccupied Parsonage as he passed by. The incident left a lasting impression on Hunter, who recounted how he continued to hear the eerie sounds even as he walked 100 yards away.
